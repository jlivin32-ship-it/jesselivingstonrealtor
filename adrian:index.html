Why Adrian?

Adrian is the largest city in Lenawee County with a population of around 20,000. As the county seat, it serves as the commercial and cultural hub for the region — with a walkable downtown, two colleges (Adrian College and Siena Heights University), and a housing market that offers real value compared to communities closer to Ann Arbor.

The city has a strong mix of historic homes, mid-century neighborhoods, and newer developments. Whether you're a first-time buyer, a young family, or an investor looking at rental properties near the colleges, Adrian has options across every price point.

Education

Home to Adrian College and Siena Heights University, bringing cultural events, athletics, and a steady rental market to the area. Adrian Public Schools and nearby districts serve K-12 families.

Downtown

A revitalized downtown with local restaurants, shops, and community events throughout the year. The Adrian Center for the Arts and the Croswell Opera House add cultural depth.

Affordability

Home prices in Adrian are significantly lower than Washtenaw County while offering comparable amenities. Your housing dollar stretches further here without sacrificing quality of life.

Location

Positioned along US-223 and M-52, Adrian offers easy access to Toledo, Ann Arbor, and Jackson. Close enough to major metros for work, far enough away for peace and affordability.
