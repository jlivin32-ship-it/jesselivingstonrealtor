About Jesse

Not Your Typical Agent

I'm a Tecumseh-based business owner who runs a residential-focused general contracting company, owns a self-storage facility, and has years of experience in large-scale commercial property management. I've remodeled my own homes, managed and renovated apartment properties, and understand buildings from the ground up.

That hands-on background means I bring a practical, investor-minded perspective to every transaction. Whether you're a first-time buyer or evaluating your next investment, I can help you see beyond the listing price to the real opportunity.
